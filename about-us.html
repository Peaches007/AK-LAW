Who We Are

The Firm is a truly unique and global law firm. Located in Van Nuys City, California, the Firm was established in 2008. The Firm currently has varied areas of expertise and combined experience in all areas of U.S. Nigeria and International law. The Firm provides a comprehensive range of specialized legal services to a highly diverse clientele from individuals, start-up visionaries, to top-tier business, governments and international corporate clients.

No doubt, our innovative approach and unmatched client service has helped guide our journey to becoming one of the world’s leading global law firms in such a short time. The Firm’s areas of specialization include, Civil Litigation, Tenant Habitability Issues/Environmental law, Personal Injury Cases (Car Accidents claims, Slip and Fall), Corporate and Commercial Law; Banking; Chapter 7 and 13 Bankruptcy filings, Mergers and Acquisitions; Telecommunications; Alternative Dispute Resolution; International Trade; International Investments; Real Estate Transactions; Energy and Natural Resources and Project / Infrastructure Finance, among others.

We have strong presence throughout the US, and Africa. We have offices in Van Nuys, Los Angeles, and Bakersfield in California. We have also developed relationships with other “Sub-Saharan” law firms in various areas of international trade and investments law and practice.

We have over the years assisted clients and also recovered and saved clients several millions of dollars. The Firm continues to expand its frontiers by providing services to U.S. based clients interested in other countries, as well as international clients with an interest in the U.S. Clients have variously described the Firm as, “a one-stop shop for legal and business solutions…”
