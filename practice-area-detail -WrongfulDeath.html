Wrongful Death

Wrongful Death: A wrongful death is a death caused by someone else’s negligent, criminal or reckless conduct. The conduct could be caused by any responsible party, including an individual, organization, company or corporation.

A legal action for a wrongful death claim belongs to the decedent’s surviving spouse and children and in some cases, siblings and parents.
