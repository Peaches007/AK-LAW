The law imposes legal responsibilities on property owners and occupiers to prevent injuries to persons while on their property.The law favors property owners by allowing them to only make “reasonable inspection” of the premises over time, so in cases of contested liability, an accident reconstruction expert is often required, who specializes in unsafe walking surfaces or tripping hazards..

Wrongful Death

A wrongful death is a death caused by someone else’s negligent, criminal or reckless conduct. The conduct could be caused by any responsible party, including an individual, organization, company or corporation. A legal action for a wrongful death claim belongs to the decedent’s surviving spouse and children and in some cases, siblings and parents.
